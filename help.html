Frequently asked
How do I find my classroom?
Use the Map tab and search for your room code (e.g. B4-312). The app will show you a route from your current location.
Why is my timetable not updating?
Timetables sync every 30 minutes. Try logging out and back in. If the issue persists, contact IT Support.
How do I report a wrong alert?
Use the "Report an issue" form above, select "Alert not received", and describe the incorrect information.
Is step-free access available everywhere?
Most blocks have lifts on the ground floor. The Map screen shows a "Step-free" badge when the route is accessible. Block C labs may have limited access.
